About Us

We Help Learners Pass The Test & Drive With Confidence

Prakash Driving School is a Dublin-based team of RSA Approved Driving Instructors (ADIs) helping learners across Ireland build safe, confident driving habits from your very first lesson.

Whether you're starting your 12 hours of Essential Driver Training (EDT) or brushing up before your test, our fully qualified instructors tailor every lesson to your pace and your local test routes.
